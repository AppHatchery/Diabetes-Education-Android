• Ketones form in the body when there is not enough insulin to carry sugar into the cells
  • Ketones are a sign that diabetes is out of balance
  • When to test for ketones
    i.When blood sugar is above 300
    ii.When child is sick- such as vomiting, diarrhea, fever

Steps to check ketones

  • Apply urine to test strip pad and wait 15 seconds. Compare the color on the test pad to the color guide on the label
  • f test strip indicates trace to small ketones, child needs to drink water and test again for ketones in 4 hours
  • If test strip indicates moderate to large ketones, call the diabetes doctor (404-785-5437) and take insulin as directed
