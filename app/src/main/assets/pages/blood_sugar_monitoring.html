• Check blood sugar at least 4 times a day before meals and bedtime
  • Check blood sugar if child is sick or experiencing symptoms of high or low blood sugar

Steps to test blood sugar

  • Gather supplies
    • Meter
    • Lancets
    • Test strips
  • Wash hands with soap and water, then dry.
  • Place test strip in meter
  • Hold the lancing device to side of finger; press button to stick
  • Apply blood to the strip according to meter directions.
  • Look at blood sugar result on meter and record result.
  • If hyperglycemia, see “giving insulin to bring down high blood sugars” in
  • how to calculate insulin dosing” section
